Wisdom of the Ascended

	Back to Map


	
Food and happiness are both essential.


	
All people know that without food comes death. It is important to search for food.


	
But you, great leader, must understand that without happiness comes defection. There are other cults in this realm who wish to guide our people astray. Those who have no more happiness become unsatisfied with their cult, and will defect to whichever cult has the most happiness.


	
The defection of followers with no happiness can be to our benefit when those from other cults join us. Keep your people happy, and not only will they remain your people, but others will join.


	
Celebration is the most straightforward way to create happiness, and it is most effective in groups. When a follower celebrates, all followers in that location become happier. Even followers of other cults start feeling better, but only a fraction as much as us true believers.


	
If we wish to convert others to our cause though, we must lower their happiness. Preach a ritual of berating the heathens to our followers, and any heathen in the same location as a follower who is berating will lose happiness.


	
You should be careful where you send your people, for some places may be dangerous to their bodies or minds. The graveyard will harm their minds and the firepit will harm their bodies, though the grove and farm will soothe them.


	
Be Productive


	
But life is more than mere survival. Preach a ritual of being productive to our followers, and they will work whatever location they are in. These lands hold bounty of all sorts.


	
The village holds potential followers. The graveyard holds jewelery. The firepit holds mystic totems. The farm holds food. And items of other sorts can be found elsewhere.


	
But being productive takes patience and dedication. It takes 30 instances of productivity in a location to yield one item. This is the sum of work put in by followers of any cult, but the prize will only go to whoever worked that location most. Upon yielding its reward, the location will reset back to 0.


	
Victory and Defeat


	
Though true triumph can only come in the next life, not in ours, someone with your wisdom and magnanimity should be concerned with bringing others along.


	
If a cult controls a high enough percentage of the population for three days in a row, they will win.


	
That goes for the other cults as well, so be warned.


	
Of course, if a cult controls the entire population, it will win instantly.


	
If you heed this advice, you will go far. Good luck.
